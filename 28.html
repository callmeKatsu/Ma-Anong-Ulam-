Kinunot na Isda Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of tanigue (Spanish mackerel)
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped (divided)
  • 4 pieces of green finger chili (siling pangsigang)
  • 2 pieces of red chili pepper (siling labuyo)
  • 1 cup of malunggay leaves (moringa), leaves only
  • 1/2 cup of vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 press of coconut milk (from 1 coconut)

Procedure:

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, boil water. Add the tanigue and ginger and boil for approximately 10 minutes. Let the tanigue cool and set it aside.
  2. Once the fish has cooled, remove the skin and crumble the meat into a bowl. Season it with the vinegar, salt, pepper, and 2 cloves of chopped garlic.
  3. In a separate saucepan over medium heat, combine the coconut milk and onion. Stir for around 3 minutes.
  4. Drain the tanigue and discard the vinegar mixture. Mix the tanigue pieces into the coconut milk mixture.

  6. Continue boiling until the liquid is reduced to half.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy this flavorful Bicolano favorite!
