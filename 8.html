Cardillong Isda Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sized fish (tilapia or lapu-lapu)
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Clean and prepare the fish by removing the scales and guts. Make diagonal cuts on both sides of the fish.
  2. Season the fish with salt and pepper.
  3. In a pan, heat the cooking oil and fry the fish until both sides are golden brown. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, saut√© the ginger, garlic, and onion until fragrant and translucent.
  5. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, and water. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the fried fish to the pan and coat them with the sauce.
    Allow the fish to simmer for additional 5 minutes.
  7. Add the sliced bell peppers and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Transfer the fish and sauce to a serving plate.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
