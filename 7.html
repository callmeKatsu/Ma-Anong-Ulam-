Rellonong Alimango Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large alimango (crabs)
  • 1 cup crab meat, shredded
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup onions, minced
  • 1/4 cup red bell peppers, minced
  • 1/4 cup green bell peppers, minced
  • 1/4 cup carrots, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil

Procedure:

  1. Clean and boil the crabs until they turn red. Remove the meat and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat the cooking oil and sauté the garlic, onions, red and green bell peppers,
    and carrots until soft.
  3. Add the crab meat and continue to cook for 2 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, combine the crab meat mixture, breadcrumbs, mayonnaise,
    soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Mix everything well until evenly combined.
  6. Stuff the crab shells with the mixture and press them tightly.
    You can also use ramekins or baking dishes if desired.
  7. Bake the stuffed crabs in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot as a main dish or appetizer.
