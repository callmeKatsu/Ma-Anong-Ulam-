Kare kare Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 kg beef
  • 1/4 kg tripe
  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup grounded toasted rice
  • 1/2 cup cooked Bagoong Alamang
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon atsuete seeds
  • 2 pieces eggplant, sliced 1 inch thick
  • 1 bundle Bok Choy (Pechay)
  • 1 bundle of String Beans
  • 1 bundle of okra
  • 3 tbsp. oil
  • 5 cups of water
  • Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Boil the Beef and Tripe in water for an hour or until cooked. Strain and keep the stock.
  2. Heat oil and atsuete seeds in a pan. Remove the seeds.
  3. Saut√© garlic, onions until golden brown.
  4. Add the stock, toasted rice and peanut butter. Add the beef and tripe.
  5. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes. Salt to taste.
  6. Add the Eggplant, String Beans, Bok Choy and Okra (If available only). Cook the vegetables for 5 minutes.
  7. Do not overcook the vegetables. Serve with Rice and Bagoong.
