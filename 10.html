Ginataang Hipon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g shrimp (hipon), cleaned and deveined
  • 1 small kalabasa (squash), peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 thumb sized ginger, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink.
  4. Add the kalabasa (squash) and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  6. Add the fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  7. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes
    until the kalabasa is tender.
  8. Stir occasionally to prevent the coconut milk from curdling.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
