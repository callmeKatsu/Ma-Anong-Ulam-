Chop suey Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 kilo broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1/4 kilo cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup chicharo (snow peas)
  • 1/4 small cabbage, cut into 1-inch thick strips
  • 1 cup Chinese pechay (bok choy)

Procedure:

  1. Saute the onion and garlic in a preheated pan with cooking oil.
  2. Stir in the pork and cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Add the green and red bell pepper, cauliflower, and carrots, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in the Chinese cabbage and snow peas.
  5. Add the oyster sauce and soy sauce, and simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. Season with salt.
