Beef Kaldereta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (700g) beef, cut into chunks (you can use beef stew meat)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon crushed peppercorns
  • 1 cup liver spread (or liver paste)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup pitted green olives (optional, but recommended)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Saute onion and garlic until onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the beef chunks and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, add the liver spread and tomato sauce. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour in the beef broth and add the bay leaves and crushed peppercorns. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef becomes tender.
  7. Add the potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and olives (if using).
  8. Cook for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked through.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice.
