Chicken Rellono Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pc whole chicken (around 1.5 kg), deboned keeping the skin intact
  • 1 cup ground pork
  • 1/2 cup ham, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup carrots, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, minced

Procedure:

  1. In a pan, melt butter then sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add ground pork and cook until browned. Add the ham and continue cooking for another minute.
  3. Add the sweet pickle relish, raisins, green peas, carrots, red bell pepper, green bell pepper,
    grated cheese, soy sauce, salt, and ground black pepper. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
    Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. Stuff the chicken with the mixture, making sure to distribute it evenly. Sew the opening to secure the filling.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  6. Place the chicken in a baking dish and roast for 1 hour or until the chicken is cooked through and
    the skin is crispy and golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy!
