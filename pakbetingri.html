Pinakbet Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 kilo pork belly, cut into strips
  • Chicharon (optional)
  • 3-4 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • Medium squash (cut into cubes)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 cups of water
  • 5 young okra, cut into halves diagonally
  • 1/2 bunch of string beans, cut into 3-inch lengths

Procedure:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics:
    • Heat oil in a preheated pot.
    • Sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until fragrant.
    • Add the pork (lechon kawali) and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Tenderize the Pork:
    • Pour water into the pot and let it boil.
    • Add a Knorr Shrimp Cube for that delicious shrimp flavor.
    • Cover the pot and simmer until the pork is tender
      (about 20 minutes for lechon kawali or 40 minutes for fresh pork).
  3. Add the Vegetables:
    • Stir in the bagoong alamang (shrimp paste).
    • Add the kalabasa (squash) and kamote (sweet potato).
      These take longer to cook, so give them about 7 minutes.
    • Next, add the remaining vegetables: okra and string beans. Cook for 5 more minutes.
    • Keep some crunchy parts of the lechon kawali to add at the end for texture.
  4. Season and Serve:
    • Season with ground black pepper.
    • Serve your Pinakbet Tagalog with warm white rice.
