Sweet & Sour Fish Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of fish fillets (e.g., tilapia, salmon, cod)
  • 1 lemon or lime, juiced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried herbs (e.g., thyme, rosemary, basil)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Optional: Fresh herbs for garnish (e.g., parsley, cilantro)

Procedure:

  1. Marinate the Fish:
    • Cut the fish into bite-sized pieces.
    • Marinate the fish in soy sauce for added flavor.
  2. Prepare the Batter:
    • Mix flour and cornstarch in a bowl.
    • Dip each fish piece in beaten egg, then coat with the flour-cornstarch mixture.
  3. Fry the Fish:
    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Fry the fish until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  4. Make the Sweet and Sour Sauce:
    • In a separate saucepan, saut√© garlic, ginger, and onion in oil.
    • Add carrots and bell peppers.
    • Pour in vinegar and dissolved cornstarch.
    • Add sugar, ketchup, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer until the sauce thickens, stirring occasionally.
  5. Combine Sauce and Fish:
    • Pour the sauce over the fried fish.
    • Toss to coat the fish evenly.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve your Sweet and Sour Fish with steamed rice.
    • Enjoy the delightful balance of sweet, tangy, and crispy flavors!
