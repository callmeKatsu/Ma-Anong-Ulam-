Chicken in Tamarind Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind soup base
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat and saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes,
    or until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the soup and set aside.
  5. Strain the soup to remove any impurities.
  6. Return the strained soup to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  7. Add the tamarind soup base, fish sauce, and soy sauce. Stir well to combine.
  8. Add the cooked chicken back into the pot.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Add the spinach leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!
