Lumpia Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup minced carrot
  • ½ cup shredded cabbage
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 30 lumpia wrappers
  • 1 beaten egg (for sealing the wrappers)

Procedure:

  1. Combine and Mix the Filling Ingredients:
    • In a bowl, mix the ground pork, chopped onion, minced garlic, minced carrot, shredded cabbage,
      chopped green onion, and soy sauce. Ensure that the filling is well-blended.
    • You can mix manually using your hands or use a stand mixer with the dough hook attachment.
  2. Wrap the Lumpia Mixture:
    • Lay out a lumpia wrapper and place about 2 tablespoons of the filling along one edge.
    • Fold the sides inward and roll tightly, sealing the edges with the beaten egg.
    • Repeat until all the filling is used up.
  3. Deep-Fry the Lumpia:
    • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
    • Fry the lumpia in batches until golden brown and crispy.
    • Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve the lumpia hot with a dipping sauce.
    • You can use a sweet and sour sauce or a garlic-vinegar dipping sauce.
